FASHION DESIGNED BY THOSE WHO SHAPE IT

Trained in the most prestigious ateliers, Pauline and Angélique bring to REBOOT a bold vision of contemporary luxury.

Here, every creation is a subtle balance between exceptional craftsmanship and responsible innovation. From vibrant ready-to-wear to personalized pieces and exclusive workshops, REBOOT celebrates the modern woman who asserts herself with style and conviction.

More than a brand, REBOOT is an experience—an invitation to live fashion differently, with transparency, commitment, and a resolutely modern elegance.

MADE FOR YOU

Ready-to-wear collection

Explore the world of ready-to-wear with REBOOT Atelier. Our wide range consists of elegant and comfortable clothing, suitable for every occasion. Discover unique pieces that combine style and quality, allowing you to fully express yourself through your outfit.

MADE BY YOU

Custom collection

With personalized clothing from REBOOT Atelier, every piece becomes unique. Choose your style and add a personal touch to your outfits. Enjoy a tailor-made service that transforms your ideas into wearable reality.

MADE WITH YOU

Couture collection

At REBOOT, couture becomes an intimate collaboration. Together with Pauline and Angélique, imagine a unique piece shaped to your desires, your body, your story. Exceptional know-how, inherited from the greatest fashion houses, at the service of a bespoke creation that reflects you, down to the last stitch.

MADE TOGETHER

Create, learn, reproduce

At REBOOT, we believe in fashion that is lived and understood.

Our couture workshops, in small groups, allow you to recreate your favorite garment or learn rare techniques of high-end sewing.

Whether you are a beginner or passionate, you will leave with knowledge, a unique piece, and the pride of having made it yourself.
